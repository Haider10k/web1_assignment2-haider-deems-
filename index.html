Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Seiko Mod?

A Seiko mod is the art of customizing a watch, where enthusiasts replace components like dials, hands, bezels, or straps to craft a unique appearance or enhance functionality. This modification process empowers our customers to personalize their watch, aligning it with their individual style, preferences, or specific needs. Each piece is hand-assembled by skilled watchmakers using a selection of genuine and high-quality aftermarket parts, bringing your vision of the perfect watch to life.

Do you Offer Warranty?

Our 1 year warranty covers any manufacturing issues with the movement of your watch, ensuring the heart of your timepiece functions flawlessly. This guarantee applies exclusively to movement-related defects, reflecting our commitment to quality and precision. While we stand by our craftsmanship, please note that we cannot assume responsibility for any damages that may occur while the watch is in your care. Wear your timepiece with confidence, knowing that our warranty safeguards its essential mechanics against any manufacturing faults.

Can I Customize my own Timepiece?

We would be delighted to help bring your vision to life. To get started, please contact us with as much detail as possible about your desired design, including preferences for components such as dials, hands, bezels, and straps, as well as any specific ideas or inspiration. The more information you provide, the better we can understand your vision and craft a timepiece that truly reflects your style.
